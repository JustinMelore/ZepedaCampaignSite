Our Vision

See what a vote for Michael Zepeda will bring for Arizona's future

Restore Fiscal Discipline While Investing in Arizona's Future

The Treasurer's Office shouldn't be a political weapon - it should be a watchdog for taxpayers. I will focus on spending smarter, not more, by cutting waste, priortizing local infrastructure, and investing in long-term growth that benefits rural and urban Arizona alike.

Lead with Civility and Integrity

Arizona is done with the drama. Our generation deserves leadership that actually works. I’ll show up with respect for every Arizonan and every lawmaker—no labels, no ego. The goal is simple: real results that make life better. Because strong ideas don’t belong to one party—they belong to all of us.

Unite Arizonans Through Nonpartisan Leadership

We can bridge the divides that hold Arizona back by listening to every community and working toward shared goals - stronger schools, thriving businesses, and sustainable water and energy management. Together, we'll build a future that works for all Arizonans, not just one side.
